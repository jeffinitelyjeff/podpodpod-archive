You probably may have already seen this coming, but here's the follow-up to Monday's episode. If you haven't listened to the first part of The Problem We All Live With, start there first.

Note: This American Life only keeps a small number of recent episodes available in their podcast feed, which means I suspect this episode will only download normally for about 2.5 weeks after posting. If you want to listen after then, you can listen (along with the rest of their archive) on This American Life's website.

Similar: This American Life has been hitting it out of the park covering stories about race this year. If you enjoyed this episode, you should definitely check out Cops See It Differently, Part One (Feb 6, 2015), Cops See It Differently, Part Two (Feb 13, 2015), and Three Miles (Mar 13, 2015).

Editorial Note: I'm unsure how to handle two-part episodes like these going forward. What I'm doing here with devoting 2/3 of the week to one story feels unfair to people who weren't interested in the first half, but I thought this particular story was gripping enough to warrant the focus. Other alternatives I've considered: just post the first episode in the feed, and link to the following parts in the show notes (but I bet a lot of people wouldn't notice and would completely miss out on the following parts); or include both parts in the feed on a single day (but this contradicts one of my main goals, which is to make this feed light and digestible). I don't really know. Let me know what you think on Twitter.
