The Bright Sessions #1
↳ Check Out: The Bright Sessions

21 Dec 2015
Listen Queue Download (19 MB)

Fiction podcasts have been on the rise lately, but I've found it very difficult to connect to most of them. But The Bright Sessions has hooked me in, with a great mix of intriguing science fiction premise, novel (but very natural) format, and serialization.

Each episode is a therapy session with a supernatural individual learning to deal with their super power (and the first season is following the ongoing sessions of 3 characters). It's a set of character studies, but it's also an interesting exploration of the consequences of flashy world-threatening powers on the emotions and turmoils of daily life.

I'm featuring the first episode because it's definitely worth starting from the beginning, and I hope you get hooked. I've really enjoyed the first 6 episodes, and I'm excited to see where this podcast goes.
