I'm not a dog person. I might go so far as to say that I hate dogs (except huskies, because wolves). And yet I found this episode all about a puppy psychologist fascinating, engaging, and hillarious.

This was also the first episode of You're the Expert I'd heard, and I'm very impressed. It seems like a really great formula for presenting interesting information with lots of engaging humor.

Original Description

Dr. Alexandra Horowitz is the head of Barnard's Canine Cognition Lab. She's also our first ever repeat guest! She was so great the first time, we had to have her on again with our new format. Panelists Mara Wilson, H. Jon Benjamin, and Josh Gondelman try to guess what Alexandra studies and then interview her about her work. Learn the truth about alpha males, guilty dog faces, and what a dog sees in the mirror.
